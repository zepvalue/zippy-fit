ZippyFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

ZippyFit ("the app", "we", "us") is a team-based fitness habit tracker. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how it is stored, and your choices. We collect only what the app needs to function — there is no advertising, no analytics, and no selling of your data.

1. Data we collect

  • Account information — your email address and password, used to create and secure your account. Passwords are stored only in hashed form by our authentication provider; we never store or see your plain-text password.
  • Profile information — an optional display name and profile image, if you choose to provide them.
  • Fitness activity — records of workouts you log (the date and an optional duration in minutes) and your team's streaks and progress.
  • Team activity — your team membership, and in-app interactions with teammates such as nudges and "spot" requests, including which member initiated them.

2. Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, calendar, files, biometric data, advertising identifiers, device identifiers, or any financial information. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

3. How your data is stored and transmitted

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored in our cloud database (provided by Convex) and processed by our backend application server. Your login session token is stored securely on your device (using the operating system's secure storage). We use these providers solely to operate ZippyFit; we do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Visibility to teammates

ZippyFit is a shared, team-based experience. Members of your team can see each other's workout-completion status, streaks, and team interactions (such as who sent a nudge). Do not join a team with people you do not wish to share this activity with.

5. Notifications

With your permission, the app may send push notifications (for example, a teammate's nudge). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing zepvalue@gmail.com from the address associated with your account. We will delete your personal data within 30 days of your request.

7. Children

ZippyFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Security

We protect your data with encryption in transit, hashed password storage, and authenticated access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: zepvalue@gmail.com.
